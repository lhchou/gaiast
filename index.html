Gaiast

We use Ideal Adsorbed Solution Theory (IAST) to predict the loading of the gas mixture on the bundle, based only on the knowledge of the pure adsorption isotherms of the individual components.

Abstract

This is an incredibly useful method, since it means we only have to run calculations or experiments for pure adsorption isotherms, and then we can predict any combination of the gases.
